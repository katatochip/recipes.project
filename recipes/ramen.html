Ramen Noods

Back to Le Recipes

Description

The tastiest noods in all the land!

Ingredients

  • preferred noods
  • pork belly
  • tonkatsu broth
  • eggy

Steps

  1. cook pork to perfection
  2. soft boil eggy to perfection
  3. cook noods in tonkatsu broth
  4. add pork and eggy to noods and broth
